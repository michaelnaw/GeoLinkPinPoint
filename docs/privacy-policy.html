Privacy Policy

GeoLinkPinPoint — Effective February 6, 2026

Introduction

GeoLinkPinPoint is a native Android utility app developed by Michael Nawrocki. This policy explains how the app handles your data.

Data Collected

The app accesses the following data:

  • Precise location (GPS coordinates) — used to display your current position and to calculate distance and bearing between two points.
  • Device sensors (accelerometer and magnetometer) — used to display a compass heading.

How Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device in a Room database. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include any analytics, advertising, crash reporting, or other third-party SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

CSV Export

You can export saved measurements as a CSV file via Android's share sheet. This action is entirely user-initiated. The exported file is shared only with the app or service you choose in the share sheet.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual measurements by swiping them in the History tab. Uninstalling the app removes all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. It does not require account creation or collect personal information beyond device location.

Changes to This Policy

Any updates to this policy will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
