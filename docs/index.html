GeoLinkPinPoint

Capture GPS coordinates from shared locations and measure distances.

Features

  • WhatsApp integration — Tap a shared location in WhatsApp and open it directly in GeoLinkPinPoint
  • Distance and bearing — Measure the distance and direction between two points instantly
  • Manual coordinate input — Enter coordinates by hand or use your current GPS location
  • Live GPS display — See your current latitude, longitude, altitude, and accuracy
  • Compass — Smooth compass with bearing-to-target indicator
  • Measurement history — Save measurements locally, tap to reload, swipe to delete
  • CSV export — Share your measurement history as a CSV file
  • Privacy first — All data stays on your device. No accounts, no analytics, no ads
