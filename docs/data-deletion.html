Data Deletion

GeoLinkPinPoint — Effective February 6, 2026

Local-Only Storage

GeoLinkPinPoint stores all data locally on your device. No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party. Because of this, you have full control over your data at all times.

Delete Individual Measurements

To delete a single saved measurement, open the History screen and swipe left on the entry you want to remove. The measurement is permanently deleted from the local database.

Delete All Data

To delete all data stored by the app, simply uninstall GeoLinkPinPoint from your device. This removes the local database and all saved measurements.

No Server-Side Data

No data is retained by the developer, as no data ever leaves your device. There is no account to delete and no server-side data to request removal of.
